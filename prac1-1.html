N.G Acharya & D.K Marathe College

N.G Acharya & D.K Marathe College of Arts ,Science & Commere was established in 1978 by chembur Trombay education society.It is ideally located in Chembur

It is first degree college in CHEMBUR.It conducts HSC(12th) courses in Arts/Science/Commerce in Junior College

The college offers Post graduate courses like MscI.T ,MCom,andMsc(Chemistry).It serves more then 8000 students each year in Junior,Degree and other courses.
